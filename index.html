WE serve the community by demonstrating scholarship, leadership, service, and character.

NHS is a student-led organization that commits to providing service opportunities with a goal to grow leaders and an engaging community.

Service? Why should I make the effort to join NHS if I can do service in my own time?

Having time commitment to investing in acts of service will net character development and develop leadership skills. NHS adds the extra benefit of being in a high school community with like-minded individuals. As a club that only meets once a month, you can attend other clubs afterschool and focus on service outside of it (I'm in Chess Club!). While most of your hours are earned outside of school, our NHS meetings offer a way to earn some service hours with fun events (also, snacks at every meeting!).

This NHS thing actually sounds kind of fun. How do I sign up?

  1. Sign up ->here<- (due 11/03)
  2. Pay your dues ($15) to the cashier by (DATE!). Free or reduced lunch students will have their dues waived. Dues help pay Club Renewals every year + finding resources and opportunities
  3. Make sure you attend the meetings (let us know if you cannot make it)!
